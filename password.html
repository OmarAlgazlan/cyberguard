💡 The password anxiety paradox

Many people feel overwhelmed by password requirements — complex rules, frequent resets, too many accounts. This anxiety often leads to the worst outcome: password reuse. The solution is not more memorisation, it's the right tools.

✓Use a password manager (Bitwarden is free and open source)
✓Enable MFA on every account that offers it
✓Use passphrases (e.g. "Purple-Banana-Telescope-42") — easy to remember, hard to crack
✓Check haveibeenpwned.com — knowledge replaces anxiety
