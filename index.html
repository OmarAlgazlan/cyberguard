Why do we feel anxious online?

Online scams and attacks are specifically designed to make you feel panic, urgency, and fear. That feeling is not a sign you're weak, it's exactly what the attacker is counting on.

Most online safety training just tells you facts. CyberGuard does something different: it lets you practise in a safe environment until the anxiety fades and confidence takes its place.

How it works, 4 simple steps

01 / RATE

Rate your anxiety

You rate how worried you feel about online threats right now, on a scale of 1 to 10. This is your starting point.

02 / PRACTISE

Work through scenarios

You'll face realistic situations, a suspicious email, a scam phone call, a dodgy message. You choose what to do. No pressure, no time limit.

03 / LEARN

Get calm, clear feedback

After each choice, you see exactly what was happening and why. Guidance is written to reduce stress, not add to it.

04 / MEASURE

See your improvement

Rate your anxiety again at the end. See your before-and-after score, most people feel noticeably calmer after a single session.
