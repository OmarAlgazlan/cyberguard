💡 Why social engineering works — and why it's not your fault

Social engineering exploits cognitive biases that are features, not bugs, of human psychology. The respect for authority, the desire to help, the response to urgency — these are normal human traits. Attackers weaponise them deliberately. Recognising this removes self-blame and replaces it with awareness.

✓Always verify identity through a separate, independent channel
✓Urgency from an unknown source is a red flag, not a reason to act
✓It's always acceptable to say "I need to verify this before I proceed"
✓If something feels wrong, trust that feeling and pause
